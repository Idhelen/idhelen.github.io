How to Create a Project on Google Earth    


Step 1: Sign into your Google Account on earth.google.com         

Go to earth.google.com and sign into your google account.

When you sign-in your web page should look like the picture presented.  

warningIt is important to note: If you do not have a Google account then you will have to create one then proceed to sign-in to begin to create your project.


Step 2: Creating a New Project         

On the right hand side of your web page click +New.

Then select New map option as seen in picture provided.  


Step 3:Title Your Project          

On the right hand side of your web page click Unitled Project and rename as seen in the picture provided.

Once you have named your project you can begin to add locations to your Google Earth project.  


Step 4:Add Locations          

On the right hand side of your web page click search bar and find the location you would like to add to your project.

Make sure you select the correct location you would like as seen in the picture provided. Once you select the location it will then be added to your map contents. Follow this step for each location.  


Step 5: Project Created          

Congratulations you have now made a personalized project on Google Earth! These projects can be useful when planning a trip or sharing a trip itinerary with friends and family.
